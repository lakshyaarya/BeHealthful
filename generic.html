Tips to Enhance Your Fitness

Do physically work 1hr or more daily. it's include yoga, sports etc.

Eat a well-balanced, low-fat diet with lots of fruits, vegetables and whole grains. Reduce salt and sugar intake.

Brush your teeth daily in the morning and night after dinner

Use seatbelts during driving and helmets on bike driving. Use safety precaution at all times

Maintain a healthy body weight.

Eat nuts.

Avoid processed junk food

Make sure to eat enough protein.

Avoid alcohol .

Be involved in your community.

Stay in touch with family and friends

Get enough sleep and rest. Adults need around 7 to 9 hours of sleep per night.
